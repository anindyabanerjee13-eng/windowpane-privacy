# Privacy Policy for WindowPane

**Last Updated: March 20, 2026**

WindowPane ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how your data is handled when you use our Chrome Extension.

**1. Data Collection and Storage**
WindowPane operates locally on your device. The Extension does not collect, transmit, distribute, or sell your personal data, browsing history, or analytical data to any external servers. 

**2. Local Storage**
To function properly, the Extension uses Google Chrome's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`) to save:
* Your custom "Saved Workspace" templates (URLs you explicitly choose to save).
* Your Pro license verification status.
This data never leaves your local machine and is not accessible by the developer.

**3. Third-Party Services (Gumroad)**
If you choose to upgrade to WindowPane Pro, the Extension will make a direct, secure API request to Gumroad (api.gumroad.com) solely to verify your license key. The Extension does not process or store your payment information. All transactions and billing details are handled entirely by Gumroad. Please refer to Gumroad's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle payment data.

**4. Permissions**
The Extension requires the following core browser permissions:
* `tabs` & `windows`: Required to read the URLs of the tabs you select and rearrange them into a split-screen layout.
* `storage`: Required to save your Workspace templates and Pro status locally.

**5. Contact**
If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store support portal or via GitHub.
